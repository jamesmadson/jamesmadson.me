Treadhub is a responsive web app my friend and I created to enable endurance athletes and their coaches to keep all their training data in one convenient place. Most digital training logs are kept in many different places: personal blogs, GPS watch accounts, social training sites, and separate coaching platforms. Treadhub combines all of these outlets into one, giving athletes more time to focus on what they do best.
