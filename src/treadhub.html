Treadhub was one of my many side projects, but unlike the others, it passed the validity test: people wanted it.

We launched our beta more than a year after working on the product: a responsive web-based app that athletes and their coaches could use to track their training and tell their friends and the world about their fitness goals and progress.

Nick, my friend and developer/engineer, and myself worked on nights and weekends giving our spare time to Treadhub. We learned a lot about business plans, product design, new technologies and much more. It's still available for our loyal users who enjoy tracking all their training in one place.
