About

Hey! I’m James Madson, an independent digital designer living in Flagstaff, Arizona. Over the last 10+ years I’ve gotten to work alongside various organizations, from small startups to global brands.

My focus is mostly on web-based products, working solo and with teams, crafting interactive user experiences while also developing new processes within organizations.

I enjoy all aspects of product design, from jumping into front-end code to making paper and digital prototypes, as well as conducting user research, coaching design teams, meeting with and presenting to stakeholders, bridging design and business goals – and even bringing donuts to meetings.
