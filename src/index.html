Swiftkick

A prototype to test whether sedentary government employees from 0 to 30 minutes of exercise a day using a system already in their workflow – Google calendar. Users could sign up, choose a move pack plan and be back at work with short activities added into their calendar at common convenient times through the day. The results proved very successful in a large majoity of the test subjects becoming more active and feeling better throughout the day.
