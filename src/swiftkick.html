Swiftkick is an app designed to help sedentary government employees add up to 30 minutes of exercise into their day using a system already utilized in their workflow: Google calendar. With this prototype, users were able to easily sign up, choose a “Move Pack Plan,” and go back to work knowing that short activities were being added into their calendar at convenient times throughout the day. The results proved very successful as a large majority of the test subjects reported that they became more active and felt better throughout the day as a result of these reminders.
